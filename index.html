PUBLICATIONS
  1. Privacy and Polarization: An Inference-Based Framework    Online Appendix    Management Science, forthcoming
      with Tommaso Bondi and Omid Rafieian.
      Extended Abstract at EC'24 (25th ACM Conference on Economics and Computation)
    Privacy regulations that ban behavioral ad targeting incentivize media firms to shift toward more extreme and polarizing positioning to draw better inferences about consumer types. Competition increases such inference motives.
  2. Reputation for Privacy    Online Appendix    Marketing Science, 2025
    This paper studies whether firms’ reputation consideration can serve as a commitment device for privacy protection in a long-run game when consumers have imperfect monitoring technology.
  3. Search Fatigue, Choice Deferral, and Closure    Online Appendix    Marketing Science, 2025
      with Z. Eddie Ning and J. Miguel Villas-Boas.
    Varying search costs can lead to choice deferral and choice closure behavior, with effects of strategic pricing and retargeting.
  4. Dynamic Persuasion and Strategic Search    Online Appendix    Management Science, 2024
    This paper endogenizes the consumer’s information environment from the firm’s perspective and endogenizes the search decision from the consumer’s perspective.
  5. A Dynamic Model of Optimal Retargeting    Marketing Science, 2021
      with J. Miguel Villas-Boas.
    The optimal retargeting strategy requires dynamic analysis, with better information tracking potentially leading to less retargeting.
